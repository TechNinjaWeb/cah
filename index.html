Browser Against The List
Cards Against Humanity Hack

Steps

  1. Choose Special Play Options Begin Game
  2. Draw 10 White "Answer" Cards
  3. Choose A Card Czar && Special Play Options
  4. Give Black “Question” Card to Czar
  5. Card Czar Reads Question
  6. Players (Not Czar) Discard 1 White Answer Card && Special Play Options
  7. Card Czar Reads Each Question In sequence with filled in answers
  8. Special Play Options determines winner of 1 Awesome Point
  9. Loop Step #2 Until 10 points || time limit reached

Output Window

White Cards = [noun, gerund, sing word, phrase constructors]

Black Cards = [fill in the blank, direct question]


Special Play Options

  • Card Czar Game Selection
  • Exchange Point For New Card
  • Vote Based Winner Selection

Modifyers

  • Pick 2 Cards
  • Pick 3 Cards
